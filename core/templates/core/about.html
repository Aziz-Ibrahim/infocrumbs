About InfoCrumbs

InfoCrumbs is a knowledge aggregation platform that delivers personalized, bite-sized content—"crumbs"—based on your selected interests. It's designed to make daily learning easier by tailoring content to your preferences, encouraging consistent engagement and lifelong curiosity.

The project was built as part of a full-stack web development course at Code Institute , applying Django, REST APIs, Bootstrap, and other modern web technologies.
